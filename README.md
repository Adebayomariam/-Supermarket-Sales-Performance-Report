# Supermarket Sales Performance Report (2020–2025)
Analyzing 3 months of supermarket transaction data to uncover sales trends, customer behavior, and profitability insights using Power BI.

## Executive Summary
Supermarket management lacked a centralized system to monitor sales performance, customer purchasing patterns, and product category contributions over time. Decision-making relied heavily on fragmented and static reports.
To address this, I developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that consolidates thousands of transaction records into a single, dynamic reporting solution.
The dashboard enables stakeholders to:
- Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) in real time
- Track revenue trends across years, regions, and product categories
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior and payment preferences
The analysis revealed key insights such as strong growth in high-demand product categories, balanced payment method usage, and specific regions driving the majority of revenue. These findings support data-driven decision-making to improve operational efficiency and profitability.

## The Business Problem
The supermarket required a data-driven approach to evaluate its sales performance between 2020 and 2025.
The main challenge was transitioning from static, disconnected reports to an interactive dashboard that provides real-time insights and supports strategic decision-making.
Key Questions Addressed:
- How have sales and key performance indicators changed year-over-year?
- Which product categories are driving the most revenue?
- What are the most preferred customer payment methods?
- Which locations or regions contribute the most to overall sales?
